# Privacy Policy for Newsily

**Last Updated:** [12/07/2025]

Newsily, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our basic features. However, we may collect:
- Device information (model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
- IP address
- App usage data and preferences
- Search queries within the app

### 1.2 News Content and Preferences
- News articles you read
- Categories you interact with (business, technology, sports, etc.)
- Reading history (if you enable this feature)
- Bookmarked/saved articles

### 1.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies
We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze app usage patterns
- Improve our services

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Personalize your news feed based on your interests
- Improve our services and develop new features
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. News Sources and Third-Party Content

### 3.1 External News Sources
The App aggregates news from various third-party sources. We are not responsible for:
- The accuracy of news content from external sources
- Privacy practices of linked websites
- Content on external news websites

### 3.2 API Usage
We use news APIs (such as NewsAPI.org or similar services) to fetch news content. These services may collect:
- Your IP address
- Request timestamps
- Requested news categories

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information:
- **With Service Providers:** Analytics providers, hosting services
- **For Legal Reasons:** To comply with laws or legal process
- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly agree to share data

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your reading history with advertisers
- Use your data for targeted advertising within the App

## 5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit
- Secure server infrastructure
- Regular security assessments

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Delete your reading history and preferences
- Opt-out of data collection (through device settings)
- Disable cookies through your browser settings

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside your country, where data protection laws may differ.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
